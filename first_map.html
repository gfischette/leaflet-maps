My Favorite Restaurants in Saint Petersburg, FL

Saint Petersburg is a city located in the Tampa Bay area of Florida. This city offers a variety of restaurants with many types of cuisine. The map below displays my four favorite restaurants in the area.

Mazzaro's Italian Market is both a market and a restaurant. Customers can spend hours shopping for handmade pasta and then sit down for a nice meal at the deli or enjoy a pastry at the bakery.

Casita Taqueria is a staple in Saint Petersburg. With three different locations in the city, it offers delicious and authentic Mexican cuisine with tacos, burritos, tortas and more.

Brick and Mortar is located in a fun night-life location next to a variety of popular businesses, including the Saint Pete Brewing Company. It offers diners an enjoyable experience in its rustic themed building. It serves New American style food and offers a wine bar for a nice night out.

Spinners Rooftop Grille is located on St. Pete Beach. Diners sit 12 floors above the shoreline and slowly spin around to look out at the beach from all angles. Spinners offers a unique dining experience that allows you to view the beach while eating delicious food.
