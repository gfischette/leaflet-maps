National Sites to Visit in California

All data from this map is from the National Park Service website. To view the other historic trails that span multiple states, click above to learn more about them!

California is well-known for many different things, including Hollywood, Los Angeles and Disneyland. As one of the top tourist locations, it draws flocks of people in every year. In 2022 alone, there was over $134.4 billion spent on travel-realated spending for California.

While California has many popular commercial attractions for people to visit, it also has many national sites, including historical monuments and national parks, for people to visit. Above is a map of all the national sites that are in California. While there are a few historic trails that run through other states as well as California, you can visit all the national sites highlighted above and stay only in California. Explore the history and nature of California with these sites!
